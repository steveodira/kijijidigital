Get Involved with Kijiji Digital

At Kijiji Digital, we believe that change happens when people come together with a shared vision. There are many ways you can contribute to our mission of empowering youth and young girls in rural Kenyan villages. Whether through fundraising, volunteering, or offering your expertise, your involvement can make a world of difference.

Fundraise for Kijiji Digital

You have a network that can change the world. Help us expand our impact by sharing the message of Kijiji Digital with those around you. Whether it’s running a birthday fundraiser on Facebook, organizing a campaign to raise donations, or hosting your own Kijiji Digital event, there are countless opportunities for you to get involved and support our cause.

We can only be successful through the partnerships we build with people like you. At the heart of our work is the deep connection we have with the communities we serve. Through fundraising, you help us share hope and provide a sense of possibility to those who didn’t know it was still out there. By contributing, you allow us to continue our work and spread awareness to others who might be inspired to join this mission.

Will you join in and help us change lives?

Volunteer with Us

There are plenty of ways you can contribute to the growth of Kijiji Digital. Whether it’s helping teach digital tools like ChatGPT, setting up computers, sending thank-you notes to our generous donors, or assisting with email communication, we always welcome volunteers eager to make a positive impact.

For those with specific expertise, we also offer opportunities for mentorship and coaching. Share your skills, whether it’s providing insights on AI technology, offering career advice to young women, or guiding our students through digital literacy programs.

There’s always a place for you to contribute your time and talent to the work we do.

Expertise Volunteering

If you have a skill set that can help us grow—whether it’s in digital technology, education, or any other field—consider offering your expertise. Your insights, recommendations, and mentorship could have a lasting impact on our mission and the young people we serve. Volunteers with expertise help us stay innovative and continually improve the quality of the programs we offer.

Email us at kijijidigital@yahoo.com to get started today and find out how you can make a difference in our work.
